# STARK Transfer Micro-interactions & Light Mode Strategy

## Phase 1: Transfer Micro-interactions
Focusing on the "Elite" banking experience through tactile and visual feedback.

### 1. The "Velocity" Keypad (Interaction)
- **Visual**: Glassmorphic keys that "bloom" with a subtle gold glow on press.
- **Micro-copy**: Amount text scales slightly as digits are entered, with a smooth ticker animation for the decimals.
- **Feedback**: A subtle progress ring around the "Confirm Transfer" button that fills as the amount is entered.

### 2. The "Pulse" Processing State (New Screen)
- **Visual**: A central, rotating STARK "S" monogram or shield logo.
- **Animation**: Concentric rings pulsing outward in gold and silver gradients.
- **Context**: "Encrypting Transaction..." and "Securing Funds..." status text that cycles smoothly.

### 3. The "Success" Reveal (New Screen)
- **Visual**: A clean, celebratory screen.
- **Component**: A 'Success Card' that slides up with a subtle bounce.
- **Details**: Checkmark icon that draws itself in a gold stroke.
- **Action**: "Done" button and "Share Receipt" option.

## Phase 2: STARK Light Mode Translation
Translating "Elite" from dark charcoal to a "High-End Gallery" aesthetic.

### Visual Mapping
- **Surface**: Pure white (#FFFFFF) to Soft Alabaster (#F8F8F8).
- **Primary Text**: Deep Onyx (#131313).
- **Accents**: Retain the STARK Gold (#D4AF37) for highlights.
- **Elevation**: Shift from glows to crisp, thin hairlines and soft, expansive shadows (natural sunlight feel).
- **Cards**: Minimalist white cards with internal borders rather than heavy containers.
